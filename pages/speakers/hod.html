Dr. K. H. Phani Shree
Professor & Head of the Department, Electrical & Electronics Engineering, JNTUH UCESTH

Dr. K. H. Phani Shree is Professor & Head of the Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering at JNTUH University College of Engineering, Science & Technology Hyderabad. She completed her B.Tech in EEE from Bapatla Engineering College, Bapatla, M.E. (Power Systems) from NIT Tiruchirappalli, and Ph.D from JNTU. She has over 22 years of teaching and research experience and is a recipient of the Engineering Impact Award (2016) from National Instruments. She developed the research laboratory in the Department of EEE at JNTU, has presented around 30 research papers in national and international conferences, and published about 12 journal papers. She has guided more than 40 M.Tech and 30 B.Tech projects and is currently supervising Ph.D candidates. She is a member of ISTE and serves as Coordinator for the Entrepreneurship Development Cell at JNTUH UCESTH.

Areas of Interest:
✔ Control of power converters for renewable energy systems
✔ Drives and electric vehicles
✔ Control of FACTS devices
